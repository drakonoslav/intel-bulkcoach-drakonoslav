	3.	Deload — volume reduction + Zone 2 dominance + neural reduction

Phase detection (non-negotiable)

The engine must infer phase from rolling signals:

Enter Deload when any combination hits:
	•	HRV 14–28d slope negative AND

Extend Accumulation/Expansion only if:
	•	FFM trend positive confidence-weighted AND
	•	weighted strength trend positive AND
	•	virility stable AND
	•	autonomic markers stable

“Arc reset” rule

Resensitize must last until:
	•	HRV stops compressing
	•	RHR normalizes
	•	virility trend stabilizes
	•	monotony decreases

Then arc restarts at Accumulation, regardless of what day-of-month it is.

This is the key puberty-mimic principle:
