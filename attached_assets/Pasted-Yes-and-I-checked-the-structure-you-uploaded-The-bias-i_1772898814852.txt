The clean fix

Do not let collective groups act as source-scored muscles if their children are already source-scored.

Use this rule:
	•	leaf nodes get matrix values
	•	group nodes are derived after computation

So:
	•	Front/Anterior Delt, Side/Lateral Delt, Rear/Posterior Delt stay as source inputs
	•	Upper Traps, Mid Traps, Lower Traps stay as source inputs
	•	Deltoids and Traps become derived rollups, not seeded channels

For lats, with your 27-region constraint, leave Lats as a leaf node and derive:
	•	Back Total = Lats + Upper Back + Middle Back + Lower Back

In runtime / backend

After all leaf-region math is computed, derive:

deltoids = front_anterior_delt + side_lateral_delt + rear_posterior_delt
traps = upper_traps + mid_traps + lower_traps
back_total = lats + upper_back + middle_back + lower_back

Use those derived values for:
	•	dashboard group summaries
	•	recovery rollups
	•	coaching summaries
	•	balance summaries

But do not feed those group values back into the per-set seed logic.

How to handle each layer

For:
	•	total dose
	•	direct dose
	•	bottleneck burden
	•	dynamic load
	•	stability load
	•	phase-specific dose

Use:
	•	sum of leaf-region outputs

For:
	•	readiness / freshness / fatigue percentages

Use:
	•	a weighted mean of the child regions, ideally weighted by recent direct dose or total dose.

That keeps group-level readiness from becoming misleading.
